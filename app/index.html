Cinémiles is a full native Android app allowing you to keep a journal of all the movies you've seen and to get stats about them. You can also rate and review them using API based on Vodkaster. You can browse the movie collection with a research, or simply scan your ticket in case you're about to watch a movie in theater.
Each movie give you miles and some of the movies can give you the opportunity to complete rewards based on context, thematic or even actors or directors aroundthe movie.
The aim was to create a complete review of all films you watch and bring you statistics about them. Furthermore, we tryed to encourage people to go to see movies in theater instead of using piracy.
Soon available on Android Market.

End of studies project at Les Gobelins developed in 3 months. MOURN is a sensitive and intimistic gameplay experiment reaching two people who don't know each other during an adventure which will bring them to play together in several ways to go on. The experiment face the players to the renunciation to accomplish everything by themselves and place them in situations where cooperation is the key to keep going through the levels.
Beyond an action/adventure game, the game engage a meeting and a discovering of the other player where improve a communication is the key. MOURN face the players to an universe full of misteries and dangers where sound is the real language between the two players.

Made with Bertrand Cayla - Jordan Delcros - Jérémy Lefebvre - Nicolas Vittori
